## Overview

The Fhish Shopping App provides a seamless e-commerce experience for shoppers using-blockchain technology.

### Key Features

- **Product Discovery**: Browse cross-merchant products and categories.
- **Unified Checkout**: A single checkout flow for multiple items across the network.
- **Loyalty & Rewards**: Integrated reward points for active shoppers.
- **Support for All Fhish Assets**: Pay with stablecoins, WETH, or BNB.

### User Experience

- **Non-Custodial**: Shoppers keep control of their assets throughout the discovery and purchase process.
- **Efficient**: Sub-second product filtering and fast checkout with minimal blockchain friction.
- **Multichain**: Support for assets across several networks (where supported).

### Technical Integration

- **Frontend**: Next.js (App Router)
- **State Management**: React Query
- **Data Source**: MongoDB (products, categories, transactions)
