Welcome to Fhish! This guide will help you get set up with our yield vaults and credit terminal on the Ethereum ecosystem.

## Prerequisites

- An Ethereum-compatible wallet (e.g., MetaMask).
- Supported assets (USDC, ETH, etc.) for deposits on Sepolia/Mainnet.
- Gas for transaction fees on the target chain.

## Quick Start

1.  **Connect Wallet:** Navigate to [app.polaris.network](https://app.polaris.network) and connect your wallet.
2.  **Deposit Assets:** Select a vault and deposit your liquidity to start earning yield.
3.  **Unlock Credit:** Use your vTokens as collateral to borrow USDCx instantly via the Credit Terminal.
