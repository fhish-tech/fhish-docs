---
title: The FHE Gateway
description: The specialized decryption and key management service.
---

The FHE Gateway is a core component of the Fhish ecosystem. It is responsible for:

- **Key Management**: Securely storing and serving the FHE public keys.
- **Decryption**: Performing decryptions of FHE results upon authorized requests.
- **Privacy**: Ensuring that only authorized results are ever decrypted.
